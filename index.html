Privacy Policy

Nudge — Habit Tracker · Effective February 10, 2026

Your Data Stays Yours

Nudge stores all habit data on your device and, if you choose to enable iCloud, in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. The developer has no access to your data at any time.

No Tracking

Nudge does not track you. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party services embedded in the app.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Specifically:

  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reporting sent to the developer
  • No server-side accounts or logins

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Nudge uses Apple's CloudKit private database to sync your habits across your devices. This data is encrypted by Apple and stored in your personal iCloud account. The developer cannot read, access, or decrypt this data.

Notifications

All notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No push notification servers are involved. Notification preferences are stored locally using UserDefaults.

Backups

The backup and restore feature exports data to a local JSON file on your device. These files remain under your control and are never uploaded to any external server.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The app does not collect data today and there are no plans to change that.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at vadym.sidorov@outlook.com.
