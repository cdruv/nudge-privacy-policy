Common Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your habit data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, it also syncs privately via Apple's CloudKit. The developer has no access to your data.

How do I sync across devices?

Make sure iCloud is enabled for Nudge on all your devices. Your habits will sync automatically through your private iCloud account.

How do I back up my data?

Use the backup feature in the app settings to export your data as a local JSON file. You can restore from this file at any time.

How does Apple Health integration work?

Nudge can read data from Apple Health (steps, workouts, mindful minutes, Activity Rings) to auto-complete habits. Grant permission in Settings and your health-linked habits will update automatically. Nudge never writes to Apple Health.

How do I manage notifications?

You can set reminders for each habit individually. All notifications are scheduled locally on your device — no external servers are involved.
